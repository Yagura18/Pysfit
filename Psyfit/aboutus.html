About Us

Psyfit is a mental wellness app created by Sahyam Chakraborty, Anirvinya Sharma, Kaushal Ayarekar, Sujeet Kumar and Yatharth Kumar.

We are a group of enthusiastic IT students whose vision behind creating this site was to help people with mental issue get a platform where they can talk to strangers going through similar situations in their life and can find a safe place where they can talk about their issues without giving up their identity or any kind of personal issues.
